Questions Before You Book?

The most common things people ask before getting on a call with us.

Do we need technical staff to work with you?+

No. We’ve built systems for businesses run entirely by non-technical people. You’ll need someone who understands your business well enough to explain it to us. That’s it. We handle the technical side.

How does the refund guarantee work?+

If we deliver the agreed system and your team genuinely isn’t satisfied with it, we refund everything you paid. We agree upfront on what success means for your project. If we hit those criteria and you still want a refund, we’ll discuss it. If we don’t hit them, there’s no discussion needed.

What happens on the free call?+

It’s a 30-minute call. We’ll ask about your biggest operational headache, what tools you use, and roughly what budget you’re working with. Then we’ll be honest about whether AI can help and what it would cost. You won’t get a sales pitch. You’ll get a direct assessment.

Can you integrate with our existing tools?+

Usually yes. We connect to most CRM platforms, ERP systems, messaging tools, and e-commerce platforms. If you’re on a less common system, we’ll check if it has an API. Most business software does. If it doesn’t, we’ll tell you upfront rather than discovering it mid-project.
