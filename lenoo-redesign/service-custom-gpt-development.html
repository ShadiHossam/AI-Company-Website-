What Is a Custom AI Assistant?

A custom AI assistant is a conversational AI system trained specifically on your business’s data and configured to behave according to your rules. It knows your products, your pricing, your policies, and your tone of voice. It gives answers that are accurate for your business, not generic answers drawn from the internet.

The fundamental difference from off-the-shelf AI tools is context. A generic AI tool starts every conversation from zero: it doesn’t know your company, your customers, or your industry specifics. A custom AI assistant starts from deep familiarity with your business. It has read your documentation, learned your product details, and been instructed on how to handle the situations your team actually encounters.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do we need technical staff to run it?+

No. We handle everything, your team just uses it, no technical skills required. Before handoff we train your admins on how to update the assistant, and you get full ownership of the system.

Where does the assistant get its answers?+

From your documents, policies, and knowledge, not the internet. It reads your documentation, learns your product details, and is instructed on how to handle the situations your team actually encounters, so answers are accurate for your business rather than generic.

Can it connect to our existing systems?+

Yes. A Connected API assistant goes beyond documents: it pulls live data from your CRM, checks order status, creates tickets, and updates records, all from a chat interface. API connections are set up during the build and tested against real scenarios before launch.
