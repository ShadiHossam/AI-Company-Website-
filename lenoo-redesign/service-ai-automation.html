What Is AI Automation, and Why Does It Beat Traditional Automation?

Traditional automation connects systems and moves data between them on a fixed schedule or trigger. It works well when data is clean, formats never change, and nothing unexpected happens. The problem is that most real business workflows involve messy data, human decisions mid-process, and inputs that vary from one case to the next. Those are the workflows that traditional automation can’t handle, and where AI changes everything.

AI automation adds a reasoning layer on top of the connection layer. Instead of “if X, do Y,” it’s “read this email, understand what the customer is asking, figure out whether it’s a return request or a complaint or a product question, and route it accordingly.” It can read unstructured documents, interpret intent, make decisions based on context, and handle exceptions without breaking.
