## Why AI Matters for Dubai SMEs

Artificial intelligence is no longer reserved for large enterprises. Dubai's growing SME sector is uniquely positioned to adopt AI — and businesses that move early will gain a structural competitive advantage.

## Where to Start

The most common mistake is trying to "do AI" everywhere at once. Start with one high-friction process:

- **Customer enquiry handling** — AI chatbots can handle 70%+ of repetitive WhatsApp and email enquiries 24/7
- **Lead qualification** — Stop manually reviewing every lead; let AI score and route them automatically
- **Reporting & dashboards** — Replace manual spreadsheet work with automated AI-generated reports

## Calculating ROI Before You Spend

Before committing to any AI project, answer three questions:

1. How many hours per week does your team spend on this task?
2. What is the fully-loaded hourly cost of that time?
3. What percentage of that task could AI automate?

A conservative 60% automation rate on a task taking 20 hours/week at AED 150/hour saves **AED 93,600/year**.

## What to Avoid

- Generic off-the-shelf tools that don't fit your workflow
- AI implementations without team training — adoption kills ROI
- Vendors who build and disappear (no ongoing support)
