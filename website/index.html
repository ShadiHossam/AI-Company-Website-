FAQ

Common Questions

How long does it take to build a custom AI system?

Most projects are delivered in 8–12 weeks from kickoff. A focused single-workflow Quick Win takes 4–6 weeks. We give you a firm timeline before you commit — no open-ended projects.

Do I need a technical team to maintain the AI after delivery?

No. We train your team to manage day-to-day operations and handle common adjustments. Full documentation is provided. 90-day post-launch support is included in every package, and optional monthly retainers are available.

Do you work with companies outside Dubai?

Yes — we serve businesses across the UAE including Abu Dhabi, Sharjah, and the Northern Emirates. Remote delivery is available for training and strategy projects. Our core team is Dubai-based and available for in-person sessions across the UAE.

How does the 100% refund guarantee work?

After delivery, you get a 30-day trial with your team. If you're not satisfied for any reason, request a full refund within those 30 days. We process it within 5 business days — no questions, no conditions. We're the only AI company in the UAE with this guarantee.

Do you provide training in Arabic?

Yes. All training materials, workshops, and post-training resources are available in both Arabic and English. Our AI systems are also built with bilingual support from day one — your customers and team can use either language — Arabic or English — from day one.

What's the minimum budget to get started?

Our AI Strategy Sprint starts at AED 15,000 and gives you a complete AI roadmap — which is deducted from any development project you proceed with. For implementation, the Quick Win package starts from AED 30,000. AI training workshops start from AED 8,000 for a full-day session.
