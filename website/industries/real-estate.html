What We Hear Every Week

The 3 things real estate businesses in Dubai keep telling us

"We lose leads over the weekend"

A buyer messages on Friday at 10pm asking about a 2-bed in JVC. By the time an agent replies Saturday morning, the buyer has already booked a viewing with another agency. It happens every week — and it's not because your agents are lazy, it's because nobody can monitor WhatsApp 24 hours a day.

"Agents spend more time on admin than selling"

Your top agent closes deals. But between logging calls into the CRM, preparing listing descriptions, sending follow-up emails, and chasing NOC letters — they're spending 3 hours a day on work a computer could do. That's 60+ hours of selling time lost each month, per agent.

"We have no idea which leads are actually serious"

200 leads come in from Bayut, Property Finder, and WhatsApp this month. 180 are window-shopping. 20 are ready to buy. But every agent treats them the same — spending equal time on someone browsing for fun and someone with a pre-approval letter and a moving date.
