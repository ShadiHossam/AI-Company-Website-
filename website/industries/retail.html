What We Hear Every Week

The 3 things retail businesses in Dubai keep telling us

"We ordered too much of the wrong thing again"

The buying team placed orders in August based on last year's sales data. But this year, TikTok made one product viral two weeks before Eid and you sold out in 3 days. Meanwhile, 800 units of something else are sitting in the warehouse discounted to clear. Manual buying without real-time signals doesn't work anymore.

"Every customer gets the same promotion"

Your loyalty program sends the same 15% off email to 50,000 customers. The person who buys premium skincare gets the same voucher as the one who buys budget cleaning products. You're spending AED 200,000 on promotions that half your customers ignore because they're irrelevant.

"Support is answering the same 12 questions 200 times a day"

Where's my order? Can I return this? Is this size available? Do you deliver to Abu Dhabi? Your customer service team answers these questions on loop — via WhatsApp, Instagram DMs, and website chat — all day, every day. Each one is a real person's time spent on something that has a known, scripted answer.
